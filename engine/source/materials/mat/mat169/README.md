# LAW169 — Hydrodynamic Elastic-Plastic (Steinberg-Cochran-Guinan) (`engine/source/materials/mat/mat169/`)

Steinberg-Cochran-Guinan-Lund (SCGL) strength model for metals under
extreme pressures and strain rates: shear modulus and yield strength are
functions of pressure, temperature, and melt fraction.
